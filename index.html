Why EMPATH Matters: The Call to Action

The Case of Nathalie Geralde

Despite her courage in speaking out against injustice at the "Baha sa Luneta" rally, Nathalie faced cruel online ridicule and body-shaming. This starkly shows how digital hostility can harm dignity and silence brave voices.

#ProtectDignity

The Tragedy of Emman Atienza

A student content creator, Emman faced a relentless wave of cyberbullying and threats while sharing her mental health journey. Her passing at age 19 is a heartbreaking reminder of the devastating consequences of lacking digital empathy.
